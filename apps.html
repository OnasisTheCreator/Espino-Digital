How It Works

Four phases.
One clear build.

No surprises, no guessing what comes next. You're involved at every step.

01

Map your workflow

Before we write a line of code, we understand how your business actually operates. Where the friction is, what's being done manually, and what the tool needs to replace or improve.

02

Decide what to build first

Most apps get built in phases. We identify what will have the most immediate impact, build that first, and let you use it while we build the next piece.

03

Build and test with you

You're involved throughout — not just at the end for a final review. The people using the tool every day should shape it as it gets built.

04

Hand off and stay available

You get the code and the full setup. We stay available for adjustments as your operation grows. No retainer required, no lock-in.
